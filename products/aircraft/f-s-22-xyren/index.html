F/S-22 Xyren

A next-generation multirole stealth fighter optimized for contested environments and networked operations.

About the F/S-22 Xyren

The F/S-22 Xyren is Alaria Dynamics' flagship fifth-generation multirole fighter. Designed for air superiority, precision strike, and distributed sensing, the Xyren combines advanced low-observable shaping with powerful sensors and secure data links to enable real-time battlespace awareness.

Design & Mission

Purpose-built for contested airspace, the Xyren emphasizes survivability, electronic warfare resilience, and seamless integration with allied assets. It supports a wide range of missions including air dominance, suppression of enemy air defenses, maritime strike, and intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance (ISR).

Advanced Avionics

Modular sensor suite with fused sensor data, advanced AESA radar, electro-optical/infrared (EO/IR) targeting, and an open mission systems architecture for rapid upgrades.

Capabilities

Stealth & Survivability

Low observable shaping, radar-absorbent materials, and signature management systems reduce detectability across radar, infrared and other spectrums.

Sensor Fusion

Integrated sensor fusion delivers a single coherent battlespace picture — reducing pilot workload and enabling faster decisions.

Electronic Warfare

Dedicated EW systems provide soft-kill and hard-kill support to protect the aircraft and allied formations.

Networked Operations

Secure datalinks enable cooperative engagement and distributed sensing with allied assets, unmanned systems, and ground stations.
