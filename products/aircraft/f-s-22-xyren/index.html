Sensors & Avionics

  • Radar: Shinsei Horizon-L12 AESA Radar — capable of tracking 40+ targets simultaneously and advanced air-to-ground mapping features.
  • Electro-Optical Systems: Enhanced IRST (Infrared Search and Track) for passive target acquisition.
  • Communication: Quantum-secure data links for unbreakable communications.
  • Electronic Warfare Suite: Shinsei "Ghostveil-X" — jamming of enemy radar/communications and deployment of countermeasures such as decoy drones.
  • Integrated AI: Advanced AI copilot for decision-making and real-time analysis.
  • Helmet-Mounted Display (HMD): Shinsei Vizon-X HUD for all-weather targeting; fully digital cockpit with multi-layered holographic displays.

Key Components & Modules

The Xyren combines agility and low observability using a hybrid delta-canard layout with trapezoidal stealth shaping and radar-absorbent composite materials. Internal weapon bays and modular systems emphasize survivability and sustainment.

  • Airframe: Hybrid delta-canard design with trapezoidal stealth architecture; internal weapons bays and radar-absorbent composites.
  • Stealth Capabilities: Active metamaterial surfaces and integrated cloaking systems to reduce radar, IR, and acoustic signatures.
  • Thermal Management: Advanced cooling systems to suppress heat signatures during high-performance flight.
